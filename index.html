Dear Coty Talent Acquisition Team,

I am writing to express my keen interest in the HR Business Partner Intern, Global Consumer Brands, HR Capability & HR4HR position at your Amsterdam headquarters.

I am currently transitioning from my Bachelor of Science in Business Psychology to a Master’s degree in Work and Organizational Psychology. During my studies, my focus has strongly been on change management, culminating in my thesis on “Communicative Methods and Strategies in Transformation Processes,” for which I graduated with top marks. Through my research, I concluded that structural transformations succeed only when the human element is methodically integrated. Coty’s mission to transform how HR operates globally, building future-ready capabilities while putting employees at the center of its iconic brands, perfectly aligns with my passion for the intersection of psychology, organizational development, and business strategy.

To support both your day-to-day HR operations and your strategic HR4HR agenda, I bring a diverse, hands-on background across three distinct roles:

First, during my time in Partner Management at cbs consultancy (March 2023-February 2024), I gained robust operational experience in contracting and recruiting. Managing these processes required high attention to detail and extensive system maintenance using SAP and Excel. This experience directly prepared me to support your employee lifecycle activities, maintain accurate HR trackers, and coordinate recruitment smoothly.

Second, over the past two years as a working student in Marketing and Event Management at cbs, I have honed my stakeholder management and communication skills. Organizing formats ranging from small team workshops to large-scale events with nearly 1,000 participants, while leveraging tools like Salesforce to manage client requests, has equipped me with the organizational backbone needed to drive employee engagement initiatives and internal communications at Coty.

Finally, during my internship at Peng Peng! Consultancy in the summer of 2025, I actively participated in ongoing transformation projects. By helping to design change workshops and supporting clients through structural shifts, I developed the practical capability-building skills required to meaningfully contribute to your HR4HR change management and learning initiatives.

I thrive in fast-paced environments and am highly comfortable using digital tools to structure information in a user-friendly way. I would be delighted to bring my blend of psychological insight, operational rigor, and hands-on mentality to your team starting in July.

I look forward to hearing from you.
